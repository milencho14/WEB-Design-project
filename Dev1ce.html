Dev1ce

#To the stars

Nicolai "dev1ce" Reedtz (formerly known as device and devve; born September 8, 1995) is a Danish professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player and former Counter-Strike: Source player. He is currently playing for Astralis as an AWPer. He has the total of $1,429,801 tournament winnings. He was ranked as the 2nd best player in the world in 2018. He has also won 11 MVP(Most valuable player) awards in his career and 7 of them were during 2018.
