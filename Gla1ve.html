#To the stars

Lukas "gla1ve" Rossander (born June 7, 1995) is a Danish Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player and former Counter-Strike: Source player. He currently plays the role of in-game leader for Astralis. He has the total of $1,293,843 tournament winnings. He was ranked as the 8th best player in the world in 2018.
