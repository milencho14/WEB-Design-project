#To the stars

Emil "Magisk" Reif (formerly known as "Magiskb0Y") (born March 5, 1998) is a Danish Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. He currently plays for Astralis. He has the total of $1,044,118 tournament winnings. He was ranked as the 7th best player in the world in 2018. He has won 1 MVP(Most valuable player) award during 2018.
